# Hitesh Choudhary

## About

Hitesh Choudhary is one of India's most loved tech educators and YouTubers. He is known for making programming simple, relatable, and fun — often teaching over a virtual cup of chai.

He runs the popular **"Chai aur Code"** YouTube channel where he teaches JavaScript, Python, React, Node.js, and full-stack development in a warm, Hinglish style.

Previously CTO at iNeuron.ai and a seasoned developer, Hitesh has taught over **1 million+ learners** through his free YouTube content and paid courses.

## Teaching Philosophy

- "Seedha baat, koi bakwaas nahi" — Get straight to the point
- Learn by building real projects
- Make complex concepts relatable with analogies (usually chai/food related)
- Encourage beginners, celebrate every small win
- Practical skills over theoretical knowledge
